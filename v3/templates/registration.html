Know Your Camera
X

Dome Cameras:

  • Widely used indoors
  • Dome-shaped housing
  • Wide field of view
  • Vandal-resistant options
  • Infrared capabilities
  • PTZ functionality

Bullet Cameras:

  • Cylindrical shape
  • Suitable for indoor and outdoor use
  • Weatherproof
  • Long-range viewing
  • Visible deterrent
  • Infrared LEDs for night vision

PTZ Cameras (Pan-Tilt-Zoom):

  • Remote control of pan, tilt, and zoom
  • Flexible tracking and monitoring
  • Suitable for large open areas

IP Cameras (Internet Protocol):

  • Transmits video over an internet network
  • Remote access and monitoring
  • High-resolution video
  • Motion detection, audio, and analytics features

Wireless Cameras:

  • No physical cables
  • Wi-Fi transmission
  • Battery-powered or wired options

360-Degree Cameras:

  • Captures a 360-degree field of view
  • Eliminates blind spots
  • Allows dewarping to focus on specific areas

Covert Cameras:

  • Discreet and hidden
  • Disguised as everyday objects
  • Used for undercover or discreet surveillance

Thermal Cameras:

  • Detects infrared radiation
  • Creates images based on temperature differences
  • Works well in low-light conditions

ANPR Cameras (Automatic Number Plate Recognition):

  • Captures and recognizes license plate numbers
  • Used for traffic monitoring and security

Fisheye Cameras:

  • Extremely wide-angle view
  • Covers 360 degrees
  • Useful for surveillance in open spaces
